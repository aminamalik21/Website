Established in 2002, the Department of Computer Science has been at the forefront of technological innovation and academic excellence. The department is committed to equipping students with the technical knowledge and problem-solving skills needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of computing. With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art labs, and a strong focus on both theory and hands-on experience, the department fosters a culture of curiosity, creativity, and continuous learning.

The Faculty of Computer Science Deparment is prominent among the Faculties of the University with the highly trained and experienced faculty members. Its achievement in research are the fruit of consistent efforts of its faculty and students. A number of research projects of multidisciplinary nature are being undertaken by highly experienced faculty in collaboration with National & International institutions.

Our department hosts several events throughout the year that celebrate learning, culture, and community spirit. One of the events are Annual Dinner and Annual Sports Gala.

  • Annual Dinner: A prestigious and formal gathering held annually to celebrate the accomplishments of both students and faculty. This elegant evening features award presentations, speeches, fine dining, and entertainment. It serves not only as a tribute to the hard work and dedication shown throughout the year but also as an opportunity for the entire academic community to come together, reflect, and build lasting memories.

A glimpse of Annual Dinner 2025

  • Annual Sports Gala: The Sports Gala is a spirited celebration of athletic excellence, teamwork, and healthy competition. Held every year, this energetic event showcases a variety of sports and games, highlighting the talent and dedication of our student athletes. It fosters camaraderie, school pride, and a strong sense of sportsmanship. From thrilling matches to enthusiastic cheering, the gala captures the essence of what it means to strive, compete, and grow together.
